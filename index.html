About Me

My Name is Nathaniel Keogh and I have chosen to write about mountain biking as it is something that has been central to me over the past few years.

I started mountain biking sophomore year of high school when a friend of mine invited me out to our local bike park "Duthie Hill", which featured a whole range of jumps for both beginners and experienced riders

I quickly became hooked, buying my own mountain bike and immersing myself in the culture surrounding the sport.

Since then, I have started working in the bike industry and competing in local races.
